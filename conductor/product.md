# Initial Concept

Rosário Vivo is a Catholic spiritual platform to help Catholics pray the Rosary faithfully, learn its mysteries deeply, and live a daily life of prayer and charity in communion with the Universal Church.

# Product Guide

## Vision
To provide a comprehensive digital companion for the Rosary that combines spiritual practice, theological formation, and community support in a visually sacred and intuitive experience.

## Goals
1.  **Daily Consistency:** Help users maintain a daily prayer streak and record their progress.
2.  **Deep Learning:** Offer a rich library of teachings and meditations on the Mysteries and traditional prayers.
3.  **Community Intercession:** Create a space for shared intentions and mutual prayer support.
4.  **Sacred Experience:** Provide a design that evokes the serenity and beauty of Catholic tradition.

## Core Features
- **Daily Prayer Tracking:** Log prayers with Mystery selection, intentions, and reflections.
- **Formation Library:** Access Scripture, Theology, and History related to the Rosary.
- **Traditional Prayer Guide:** Step-by-step instructions and full texts of traditional prayers.
- **PWA Support:** Installable on mobile devices for a native-like experience.
- **Mystery of the Day:** Automatic rotation with Scripture references.
- **Streak Tracking:** Monitor prayer consistency over time.
