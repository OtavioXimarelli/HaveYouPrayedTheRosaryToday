# Specification: UI/UX Refinement and Consistency

## Overview
This track focuses on a comprehensive UI and UX refinement across the entire "Rosário Vivo" application. The primary goal is to ensure a cohesive, accessible, and elegant experience that strictly adheres to the "Sacred Glassmorphism" design principles across all areas of the application.

## Functional Requirements
- **Global Consistency:** Standardize layouts, padding, and margins across all pages to ensure a unified visual language.
- **Prayer Tracking & Guide:** Refine the dashboard, check-in flow, statistics, and step-by-step rosary experience.
- **Formation Library:** Improve the readability and visual hierarchy of articles, learning paths, and history sections.
- **Global Components:** Update navbars, modals, and shared layout wrappers for seamless interaction.
- **Device Support:** Maintain a balanced, responsive design that works flawlessly on both mobile (PWA) and desktop/tablet breakpoints.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Typography & Spacing:** Standardize the use of Cinzel (headers) and Manrope (body) fonts with a harmonious spacing scale.
- **Animations:** Implement elegant, sacred-feeling GSAP transitions and interactive feedback (e.g., hover states, page transitions).
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast, proper ARIA roles, and visible focus states across all interactive elements.
- **Color & Styling:** Refine the "Cathedral Night" and "Sacred Cream" themes, optimizing the use of translucent surfaces, blurs, and metallic gold accents.

## Acceptance Criteria
- All pages share a consistent layout structure and spacing scale.
- Interactive elements provide immediate, elegant visual feedback via GSAP.
- The application passes automated and manual accessibility audits (contrast, keyboard navigation).
- The design looks and feels polished on both mobile and desktop screens.

## Out of Scope
- Adding completely new features or sections not related to the existing UI.
- Backend logic changes not required by UI updates.
