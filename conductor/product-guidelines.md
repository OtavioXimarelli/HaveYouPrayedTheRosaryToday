# Product Guidelines

## Tone & Voice
- **Reverent & Sacred:** Maintain a tone that is respectful of the Catholic faith and its traditions.
- **Encouraging & Warm:** Use language that inspires and supports the user's spiritual journey.
- **Clear & Simple:** Ensure that instructions and teachings are accessible to both beginners and seasoned practitioners.

## Design Principles
- **Sacred Glassmorphism:** Use translucent surfaces with soft blurs to evoke a sense of light and depth, reminiscent of stained glass.
- **Metallic Gold Accents:** Employ gold colors for highlights and key interactive elements to symbolize the divine.
- **Cinzel & Manrope Typography:** Use Cinzel for headers (for a classic, stone-etched feel) and Manrope for body text (for modern legibility).
- **Cathedral Night (Dark) & Sacred Cream (Light):** Offer two primary themes that reflect different spiritual moods and environments.
- **Mobile-First Responsive:** Prioritize a seamless experience on mobile devices, given the PWA nature of the app.

## UX Principles
- **Minimalist Focus:** Minimize distractions during prayer to help users stay focused on the Mysteries.
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Use a clear, icon-driven menu (staggered menu) for easy access to different sections.
- **Interactive Feedback:** Provide subtle animations (using GSAP) and haptic-like feedback to make the app feel alive and responsive.
